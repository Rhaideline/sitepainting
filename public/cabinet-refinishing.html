Cabinet Refinishing Services

Transform your kitchen and bathroom cabinets with expert refinishing—a cost-effective alternative to complete replacement.

Professional Cabinet Refinishing in Massachusetts

Are your kitchen cabinets looking tired, outdated, or worn? Mass Painters Pro specializes in cabinet refinishing and painting services that can completely transform your kitchen or bathroom at a fraction of the cost of full cabinet replacement.

Our expert craftsmen have refinished thousands of cabinets throughout Boston, Worcester, Springfield, and communities across Massachusetts. We understand the techniques and finishes that create durable, beautiful results that last for years.

Our Cabinet Refinishing Services Include

  • Kitchen Cabinet Painting Complete refinishing of kitchen cabinets in any color—from classic whites to bold modern hues.
  • Bathroom Vanity Refinishing Transform bathroom vanities and storage cabinets with durable, moisture-resistant finishes.
  • Cabinet Door Replacement Combine refinishing with new doors for a complete kitchen makeover at a fraction of full replacement cost.
  • Hardware Updates Install new handles, hinges, and pulls to complete your cabinet transformation.
  • Wood Staining & Restaining Restore natural wood beauty with professional staining and clear coat finishes.
  • Specialty Finishes Custom glazing, distressing, antiquing, and other decorative techniques to match your style.

Why Refinish Instead of Replace?

Cabinet replacement can cost $15,000 to $50,000+ for a typical kitchen. Professional refinishing delivers stunning results at 50-70% less cost while keeping your existing cabinet boxes, reducing waste, and minimizing disruption to your home.

Our factory-quality finishes are incredibly durable—we use premium catalyzed paints and coatings that resist chips, scratches, and daily wear. With proper care, your refinished cabinets will look beautiful for 10-15 years or more.

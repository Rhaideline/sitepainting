Our Services

Complete Exterior Painting Services

From siding to trim, decks to fences, we handle every exterior surface with expert care and premium materials built for New England weather.

Siding Painting

Expert painting for wood, vinyl, aluminum, and fiber cement siding with long-lasting, weather-resistant results that protect your investment.

Trim & Fascia

Precision work on window trim, door frames, fascia boards, and soffits that adds curb appeal and protects against moisture damage.

Door & Shutter

Deck & Fence

Power Washing

Caulking & Sealing

Built for New England

Weather Protection Guaranteed

Our exterior solutions are specifically formulated to handle everything Massachusetts weather throws at your home — year after year.

Winter Ready

Freeze-thaw resistant paints that won't crack or peel during harsh New England winters with temperatures well below zero.

UV Protection

Advanced UV blockers prevent fading and chalking from intense summer sun, keeping colors vibrant for years.

Moisture Barrier

Breathable yet waterproof coatings that prevent moisture damage while allowing walls to release trapped humidity.

Nor'easter Tough

Impact-resistant finishes that stand up to coastal storms, driving rain, and salt air without deteriorating.
